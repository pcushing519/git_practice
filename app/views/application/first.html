Arnold Drummond

Member since 1978

Arnold John Jackson is the younger brother of Willis Jackson (Todd Bridges), and was born in Harlem, New York City on October 19, 1971. He was practically known for his catch phrase, "Whatchoo talkin' 'bout Willis?, which became a part of popular culture.

His mother worked as a housekeeper for Philip Drummond (Conrad Bain). After Arnold's parents died, he and his brother Willis went to live with and be cared for by Mr. Drummond.

Arnold is the main character in the television series. In many episodes, he is shown as being a selfish younger brother, or coming up with or being suckered into some scheme to keep out of trouble or obtain his desire of the episode.
